Our local knowledge of Ashford

Ashford is one of the largest towns in Kent and one of the most rapidly growing, with substantial new housing on the southern and western edges and a major commercial centre served by the international rail station. The road network is well-engineered: the M20 runs along the northern edge, the A20 and A28 connect the centre, and a clear ring road system handles cross-town traffic. For removal vehicles this is generally an easy environment to work in, wide carriageways, predictable junctions and reasonable signage.

Ashford housing is dominated by modern family homes, particularly in Park Farm, Singleton, Repton Park and Kingsnorth where successive housing waves have created large planned residential areas. The town centre and Willesborough have a more traditional mix of Victorian terraces, post-war housing and newer infill developments. Each area has its own access characteristics, and we plan the vehicle and route accordingly.

Parking restrictions in Ashford are heavier in the immediate town centre, pay-and-display, residents permit zones and time-limited restrictions cover most of the streets within the inner ring road. Outside the centre, on the planned modern estates, parking is straightforward with driveway access on the great majority of properties. We routinely apply for parking suspensions from Ashford Borough Council for any town-centre move.

Ashford sees a high volume of long-distance and inter-county moves because of the international rail link and the strong M20 commuter draw. Many of our jobs involve London-to-Ashford relocations, families coming in from the wider South East, and outbound jobs to the rest of Kent and beyond. We handle the full mix as fixed-price quotes with extended journey insurance included.

On every Ashford move our team confirms the vehicle size, parking plan and any council suspension requirement in advance. For modern family homes in Park Farm, Singleton and Kingsnorth we routinely use a 7.5 tonne lorry comfortably; for town-centre and Willesborough properties on tighter streets we plan with a Luton-bodied vehicle. Where parking restrictions apply we apply to Ashford Borough Council for a temporary suspension on the loading and unloading sides of the move.

Ashford moves often link with the wider Mid Kent and South Kent commuter areas, for jobs heading west we coordinate with our Maidstone removals service, and for the M20-corridor coast-bound moves we connect with our Folkestone removals service coverage.

Did you know? Your home contents insurance almost certainly does not cover your belongings during a removal. Every move we carry out is protected by comprehensive goods-in-transit insurance covering full replacement value. We carry proof of cover on every job and provide it on request before you commit to a booking.

How we price your Ashford removal

Every Ashford quote we write is built from the actual specifics of your move, not a one-size-fits-all rate card. The factors that genuinely affect the price are the volume of your contents, the property type and access at both ends, the distance involved, the date you want to move, and any extras such as packing, furniture dismantling and reassembly, or storage. For 3-bedroom homes and larger we offer a free pre-move survey, in person or via a video walkthrough, so the quote is grounded in what is actually being moved rather than a guess from a postcode and a property size.

Once you accept the quote, the figure is fixed. We do not add charges on the day for stairs, parking waits, heavier items, additional time, fuel, or anything else. The price you sign off on is the price you pay, and any underestimate on our part is our cost to absorb, not yours. For Ashford moves specifically we factor in the M20 corridor travel time and the M20 J9 access, and the journey to and from our Rochester depot takes 40 to 50 minutes outside peak rush.

Because we operate our own crew and our own vehicles rather than subcontracting work out, our pricing stays consistently competitive against the larger national operators serving Kent, without compromising on care or on accountability for your belongings.
