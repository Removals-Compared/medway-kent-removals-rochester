Moving tips

How to prepare for your removal day

A well-prepared move is a smooth move. Here is our practical guide based on hundreds of removals across Medway and Kent.

📅

Book as early as possible

End-of-month Fridays and the summer school holiday period (July to September) book up fastest. We recommend booking at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance for peak periods.

📦

Start packing non-essentials early

Begin packing items you do not use regularly at least two weeks before your move. Books, ornaments, out-of-season clothing, spare bedding. Label every box clearly with contents and destination room.

🕒

Arrange parking for the removal van

Contact your local council well in advance to arrange a parking suspension if needed, particularly in busy Medway streets. In Rochester city centre, Chatham High Street and parts of Gillingham this is especially important.

🏠

Prepare your new property in advance

Visit your new property before moving day and plan where main items of furniture will go. Take measurements of doorways, stairwells and rooms so you know if any large items need dismantling to fit through.
