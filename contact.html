What happens next

What to expect after you contact us

We want the process of getting a quote and booking your removal to be as easy and clear as possible. Here is exactly what happens after you get in touch.

1

We review your enquiry

As soon as your quote request comes in, a member of our team reads through all the details you have provided. We look at the property size, the postcodes, the date, any access challenges and any special requirements you have mentioned. If we need any clarification, we will call you promptly. We do not send generic, vague quotes without understanding the specific job.

2

We send your detailed quote

We prepare a fully itemised, fixed-price quote that covers everything we have agreed. This includes the crew size, vehicle size, time allocation, any packing or storage services discussed, and the total price including VAT. We send this to you by email, usually within 60 minutes during business hours. There are no hidden additions after this stage.

3

You confirm and we book your date

When you are happy with the quote, you confirm the booking and pay a deposit to secure your moving date. We send you a full booking confirmation by email. As your moving date approaches, we carry out a pre-move call to go through all the final details and answer any questions. On moving day, we arrive at the agreed time and handle everything from that point forward.

Pricing guide

Understanding removal costs in Rochester and Medway

We believe in being transparent about how we price our removals. While every move is different and we always provide a bespoke quote, the following guide gives you a general sense of what to expect for common move types in Medway and across Kent. All prices are indicative and your actual quote may be higher or lower depending on the specific details of your move.

🏠

Studio or 1-bed flat

A compact local move within Medway typically requires a transit-sized vehicle and 2 crew members. Estimated range for a local move: £250 to £400 depending on access, floor level and volume of belongings.

🏠

2-bedroom property

A 2-bedroom flat or house move within Medway typically requires a Luton-sized vehicle and 2 to 3 crew. Estimated range for a local move: £350 to £550 depending on specific requirements.

🏠

3-bedroom house

A 3-bedroom house move within Medway or across Kent typically requires a 7.5-tonne vehicle and 3 crew. Estimated range: £550 to £850 for a local move. Higher for longer distances or with packing.

🏠

4 to 5-bedroom house

A larger family home requires a large 7.5-tonne vehicle or two vehicles, and 3 to 5 crew. Estimated range for a local Kent move: £900 to £1,500 and above, depending on volume and requirements.

🚎

Man and van

For single items, small loads and short moves, our man and van service in Rochester and Medway starts from £90 to £150 for a short local job. Larger jobs and longer distances are priced individually.

🗺

Long distance moves

Long distance removals from Kent to other parts of the UK are priced based on distance, property size and specific requirements. Contact us for an accurate quote for your destination.

These are general guide prices only. Your actual fixed-price quote will be based on the specific details of your move. Factors that affect price include: property size, volume of belongings, access at both properties, distance, date of move, and any additional services required.

Useful information

Preparing for your moving day

📅

Book your removal date as early as possible

Once you have an agreed completion or tenancy end date, contact us to secure your preferred removal date with a deposit. End-of-month Fridays and Saturdays in the summer fill up very quickly in Medway and across Kent. The earlier you book, the more likely you are to get your first-choice date. If you are in the early stages of a property transaction and your date is not yet confirmed, we can provisionally pencil in a date and firm it up when you have confirmation, subject to availability.

📦

Start packing non-essential items well in advance

If you are not using our professional packing service, start packing items you do not need on a daily basis as early as 3 to 4 weeks before your move. Books, out-of-season clothing, spare bedding, decorative items, DVDs and similar items can all be boxed up well ahead of time. Label every box clearly with the contents and the room it is destined for in your new property. This helps our crew load efficiently and ensures nothing ends up in the wrong room.

🕔

Arrange parking in advance for the removal vehicle

If your property is on a restricted parking street, a permit zone or a very narrow road in Medway, you should contact Medway Council in advance to arrange a temporary parking suspension for the removal vehicle. This typically requires at least 7 working days notice and involves a small fee payable to the council. We can advise you on the correct vehicle length to request based on the size of vehicle we plan to bring. Failure to arrange adequate parking is one of the most common causes of delay on moving day.

⚒

Prepare a moving day essentials box

On moving day, you will not be able to locate specific items in boxes easily once loading has begun. Before the removal starts, set aside a clearly labelled box or bag with the things you will need immediately on arrival at your new property. This should include kettle, mugs, tea and coffee, phone chargers, toilet paper, a change of clothes, medications, important documents and anything else you will need before you can unpack properly. Keep this box with you in your car rather than on the removal vehicle.

Moving checklist

Your complete moving checklist for Rochester and Medway

Use this checklist to plan your move effectively. The more you prepare in advance, the smoother your moving day will be. Our team is always happy to advise on any of these points if you have questions.

6 to 8 weeks before your move

  • Contact Medway and Kent Removals for a free quote
  • Confirm your removal date and secure it with a deposit
  • Begin sorting and decluttering to reduce the volume of items to move
  • Donate, sell or dispose of items you no longer need
  • Start collecting packing boxes if you are self-packing
  • Notify your employer, bank, HMRC and relevant organisations of your new address
  • Register with a new GP and dentist near your new address if required

2 to 4 weeks before your move

  • Begin packing non-essential items, books, ornaments, out-of-season clothes
  • Arrange a parking suspension if required for your street in Medway
  • Confirm key collection arrangements with your estate agent or solicitor
  • Arrange transfer of utility accounts at your current and new addresses
  • Set up mail redirection with Royal Mail
  • Arrange pet care or childcare for moving day if required
  • Confirm building insurance starts on completion day

Moving week

  • Defrost fridge and freezer and use up perishable food
  • Pack remaining items, keep out only daily essentials
  • Label every box with contents and destination room
  • Prepare your moving day essentials box or bag
  • Charge your phone and have key contacts ready, solicitor, estate agent, us
  • Confirm final details with our team, we will also call you the evening before
  • Pack important documents in a secure bag to travel with you personally

Moving day

  • Our crew arrive at the agreed time, introduce yourself and show us around
  • Keep children and pets safely away from the loading area
  • Make sure all access at your destination is arranged, keys ready
  • Do a final check of all rooms, loft, shed and garage before leaving
  • Take meter readings at both properties and photograph them
  • Hand over keys to your property once it is fully empty
  • Confirm with our team that you are happy before we leave your new home
Common questions

Frequently asked questions about getting a quote and booking

We aim to respond to all online quote requests within 60 minutes during business hours. Our business hours are Monday to Friday 7am to 7pm, Saturday 8am to 5pm and Sunday 9am to 2pm. For enquiries received outside business hours, we respond first thing the following morning. If your move date is imminent or your enquiry is urgent, calling us directly on 01634 971005 will get you the fastest response. We are usually able to provide an indicative quote on the phone immediately and follow up with a detailed written quote shortly afterwards.
Our quotes are fixed prices, not estimates. This means the price stated in your quote is the price you pay on moving day, with no additions for stairs, parking waits, heavy items, extra time or anything else beyond what is discussed and agreed at the quoting stage. The only exception to this is if you request additional services on moving day that were not included in the original quote, in which case we would discuss and agree any additional cost with you before proceeding. We believe fixed-price quoting is the only fair approach and it is something our customers specifically praise in their reviews.
For most moves, we are able to provide a detailed, accurate fixed-price quote based on the information you provide in your online form or over the phone, without needing to visit your property beforehand. For larger or more complex moves, such as large 5-bedroom properties, moves involving a significant number of specialist items, or commercial relocations, we may suggest carrying out a brief pre-move site visit to ensure our quote is fully accurate. If a site visit is beneficial, we will suggest it and arrange a convenient time with you. Site visits are always free of charge.
When you confirm your booking, we require a deposit to secure your moving date. The deposit amount is clearly stated in your quote. The remaining balance is payable on the day of your move, either just before we begin loading or immediately after the move is complete, depending on the specific arrangement agreed. We accept payment by BACS bank transfer, cash and all major credit and debit cards. All payment terms are set out clearly in your booking confirmation.
Property transactions in Medway and Kent frequently involve date changes. If your completion date changes, contact us as soon as you know. We will do everything we can to accommodate the new date. If we have availability on the new date, we will simply move the booking at no additional charge. If the new date is not available, we will work closely with you to find the nearest suitable alternative. We understand that date changes are rarely within your control, and we always try to be as flexible and supportive as possible in these situations.
Yes, absolutely. We regularly provide quotes to customers who are still in the early stages of a property transaction and do not yet have a confirmed completion date. We can quote based on an approximate timeframe and provisionally pencil in likely dates, then firm up the exact date when your solicitor confirms completion. This means you have your quote ready and an idea of availability, so there is no scramble to find a removal company at short notice once completion is confirmed.
