Removal services in Maidstone — everything you need to know

Maidstone is Kent's county town and one of the most significant relocation destinations in the whole of the South East. The town has expanded considerably over recent decades and continues to grow, with substantial new residential development on its outer edges while the historic town centre and surrounding established neighbourhoods maintain their character and appeal. Maidstone draws both people moving within Kent and a significant number of families and professionals relocating from London seeking more space and better value for money.

We carry out regular removals throughout Maidstone and across the entire Maidstone borough, covering the town centre and all established residential neighbourhoods including Bearsted, Loose, Shepway, Senacre, Allington, Barming, Vinters Park and Penenden Heath, as well as the outlying villages and the growing residential communities along the A20 and M20 corridors including Larkfield, Ditton, West Malling, East Malling, Aylesford and Wateringbury.

Maidstone is home to a wide variety of property types that present different logistical challenges for removals. The town has a significant number of purpose-built flat developments in and around the town centre, period properties including substantial Victorian and Edwardian houses in the more established residential areas, modern executive homes on newer estates, and everything in between. Our team has the experience and the equipment to handle all of these property types efficiently and carefully.

Our local knowledge of Maidstone

Maidstone's road network is one of the more complex in Kent for removal vehicles. The town centre one-way system, the ring road and the approach routes over the River Medway at Maidstone Bridge and the various alternative crossings require careful routing for larger removal lorries. Our drivers know Maidstone's roads thoroughly and plan routes in advance based on the specific addresses involved, avoiding the congestion pinch points that form during peak hours on the A229, A249 and A20 approaches to the town.

The Maidstone town centre and the streets immediately surrounding it have challenging parking for removal vehicles, with extensive yellow line coverage, residents permit schemes and delivery restrictions. For moves in the town centre and adjacent streets including those around Week Street, Gabriel's Hill and the surrounding residential roads, we advise customers to apply for a parking suspension from Maidstone Borough Council well in advance.

In contrast, the outer residential areas of Maidstone — Bearsted, Loose, Allington, Barming and the newer estates on the town's periphery — generally offer much more straightforward access for removal vehicles, with wider roads and easier parking. Many of the newer residential developments have dedicated visitor parking and more generous road widths that make removal day logistics significantly simpler.

Maidstone's business district, including the significant concentration of offices and commercial premises in and around King Street, Week Street and the Forstal Road area, is also well-served by our commercial removal team. We work around business hours to minimise disruption and know the loading and unloading arrangements at most commercial premises in the town.

Did you know? Your home contents insurance almost certainly does not cover your belongings during a removal. Every move we carry out is protected by comprehensive goods-in-transit insurance covering full replacement value. We carry proof of cover on every job and provide it on request before you commit to a booking.
