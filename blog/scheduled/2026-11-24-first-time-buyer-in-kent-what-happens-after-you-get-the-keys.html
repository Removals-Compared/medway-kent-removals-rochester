First-Time Buyer in Kent: What Happens After You Get the Keys

Published by Medway Kent Removals | November 2026

Getting the keys is the moment you have been working towards. What happens next is where first-time buyers are often least prepared. Here is the practical guide.

First 48 hours

Take meter readings immediately on arrival - gas, electricity, water. Photograph them. Contact the suppliers to register. Find the stopcock, the fuse box and the boiler. Check all windows and doors lock correctly. Change the locks - you do not know how many copies of the old keys exist.

First week

Register on the electoral roll at your new address. Update your driving licence address with the DVLA. Notify your bank, employer and HMRC. Set up mail redirection from your old address for at least six months. Introduce yourself to immediate neighbours - it costs nothing and pays dividends.

First month

Get a gas safety check if the property has not had one recently. Bleed the radiators. Check the loft insulation. Find out when the bins are collected and which day recycling goes out. Register with a local GP and dentist.
