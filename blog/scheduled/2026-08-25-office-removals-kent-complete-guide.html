Office Removals in Kent: The Complete Guide

Published 26 Aug 2025  |  9 min read  |  Medway & Kent Removals

An office relocation in Kent or Medway is one of the most logistically complex operations a business can undertake. Unlike a house move, a commercial relocation affects staff productivity, client relationships, IT infrastructure and business continuity simultaneously. With proper planning and an experienced commercial removal company that knows the Kent and Medway area, it is entirely possible to relocate your office with zero working-day downtime.

Kent and Medway business parks and commercial locations

Medway and Kent have a wide range of commercial property to relocate to or within. The Medway City Estate in Strood, the Chatham Maritime development and the Rochester Airfield Business Park are the main commercial hubs in the Medway towns. Maidstone has significant commercial property at Maidstone Business Park and along the A229 corridor. Tonbridge, Tunbridge Wells, Canterbury and Folkestone each have their own commercial districts. Medway and Kent Removals has experience working in all of these commercial areas and knows the access requirements, vehicle restrictions and working hour constraints specific to each.

Start planning at least three months in advance

A commercial office move affecting more than five staff should begin planning at least three months before the intended move date. Larger offices — twenty or more staff — require four to six months. Appoint a single internal project manager who owns the move from start to finish. Without clear internal ownership, decisions slow down and critical tasks are missed.

IT infrastructure is always the critical path

In almost every office move, IT is the element that takes longest to plan and is most likely to cause downtime if not managed correctly. Before anything else is decided, your IT team or managed IT provider must be involved in planning the move. Key questions:

  • How will servers be transported or migrated to cloud infrastructure?
  • What is the lead time for broadband installation at the new premises?
  • How will phone systems — particularly VoIP systems — be transferred?
  • Will any hardware need to be replaced or upgraded at the new address?

Broadband installation at commercial premises in Kent can take four to eight weeks from order. This must be booked immediately upon confirming the new address.

Weekend moves — the zero downtime solution

The most effective way to achieve zero downtime is a weekend move. Staff finish work on Friday in the old office. By Monday morning, the new office is fully set up and functional. This requires the removal team to work through Saturday, potentially into Sunday, to complete the move and the setup. Medway and Kent Removals offers weekend commercial removal services specifically designed for this scenario and regularly carries out overnight and weekend office moves across the Medway towns and Kent.

Labelling for commercial moves

Give each staff member a set of coloured labels with their name and new desk number before packing begins. Everything on their desk — monitors, accessories, personal items — goes in boxes with their name label. On arrival at the new office, each person's items go directly to their new desk without any sorting required. This simple system saves hours of confusion and speeds up the setup significantly.

Notify clients and update all contact details

Update your address on your website, email signatures, LinkedIn, Companies House, Google Business Profile and all supplier accounts at least two weeks before the move. Send a change of address notification to all active clients and key suppliers. Redirect post from the old address for at least six months.

Specialist equipment and furniture

Commercial moves often involve specialist equipment — server racks, large format printers, photocopiers, specialist workbenches or manufacturing equipment — that requires more than standard removal capability. Always declare specialist equipment when requesting your commercial removal quote so we can plan the appropriate crew size, equipment and vehicle selection. Medway and Kent Removals has experience handling specialist commercial equipment across all sectors.

Plan the new office layout before moving day

Create a detailed floor plan of the new office showing exactly where each item of furniture and equipment is to be placed before moving day. Give this to the removal team on the day. This eliminates time-consuming repositioning after delivery and ensures staff arrive Monday morning to a fully organised working environment.
