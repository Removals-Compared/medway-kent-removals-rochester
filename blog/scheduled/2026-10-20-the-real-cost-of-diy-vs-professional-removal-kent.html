The Real Cost of DIY vs Professional Removal in Kent

Published by Medway Kent Removals | October 2026

Hiring a Transit van and calling in favours from friends seems like the affordable option. Here is a realistic cost comparison for a typical three-bedroom house move in Kent.

DIY move costs

Van hire for a day: £80 to £150. Fuel: £40 to £80 depending on distance. Packing materials: £60 to £120. Pizza and drinks for helpers: £50. Lost wages if you take time off work: variable. Damaged furniture or items: potentially significant. Total realistic cost: £300 to £500 before any damage.

Professional removal costs

A professional removal for a three-bedroom house in Kent typically ranges from £500 to £900 depending on distance, access and whether packing is included. This covers trained crew, the right vehicle, full insurance and no risk to your belongings.

What DIY does not account for

The cost of a damaged sofa, scratched floor or broken television can easily exceed the difference in price. More importantly, a DIY move takes an entire weekend of exhausting physical labour. A professional move takes a day and you spend it supervising rather than carrying.

When DIY makes sense

A studio flat with minimal furniture and helpful friends nearby. A very short distance move with easy access at both ends. If you genuinely enjoy the process. For most three-bedroom-plus moves, the professional option costs less than people assume once all factors are considered.
