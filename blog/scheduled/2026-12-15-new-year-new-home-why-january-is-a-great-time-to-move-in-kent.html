New Year, New Home: Why January Is a Great Time to Move in Kent

Published by Medway Kent Removals | December 2026

Nobody thinks of January as a great time to move house. That is exactly what makes it a good time. Here is why a January move in Kent might be the smartest decision you make.

Less competition from other buyers

The January property market in Kent is quieter than spring or summer. Fewer buyers means less competition on the homes you want and occasionally more room to negotiate. Sellers who were unable to complete before Christmas are often highly motivated in January.

Removal company availability

January is our quietest month. That means you get first choice of dates, more flexibility on timing and full attention from the team. The contrast with trying to book a summer Friday move could not be greater.

New year clarity

There is something psychologically right about starting a new home at the start of a new year. The reset feels complete. Many of our January clients specifically chose this timing for this reason.

The practical considerations

January weather in Kent can be cold and occasionally icy. We plan around this. Roads are quieter on weekdays in January which makes logistics easier. Heating should be checked and working before moving day if the property has been empty.
