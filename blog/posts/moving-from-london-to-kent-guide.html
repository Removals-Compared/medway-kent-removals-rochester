Moving from London to Kent: The Complete 2025 Guide

Published 3 Jun 2025  |  9 min read  |  Medway & Kent Removals

Kent is one of the most popular destinations for Londoners relocating out of the capital. Fast rail links to multiple London termini, dramatically better property value, outstanding schools including one of the country's finest grammar school systems, and the Garden of England's extraordinary countryside and coastline make Kent a compelling choice for families and professionals making the move.

Why Londoners choose Kent

The financial case is compelling. A budget of £500,000 that buys a two-bedroom flat in Zone 3 London buys a four or five-bedroom detached family house in many parts of Kent. The grammar school system — one of the few remaining fully selective systems in England — is a major draw for families. The coastline at Whitstable, Herne Bay, Folkestone and Margate adds a lifestyle dimension that is genuinely distinct from anything available in London.

Best Kent towns for London movers

Sevenoaks

The fastest London commute in Kent — 22 minutes to Cannon Street. Outstanding schools, beautiful North Downs countryside, premium prices reflecting the proposition. Consistently the most sought-after town in Kent for London professionals.

Tonbridge

Slightly more affordable than Sevenoaks with a similar proposition. 40 minutes to London Bridge and Charing Cross. Tonbridge School is one of England's finest independent schools. Strong grammar school provision.

Maidstone

The county town with good M20 and rail access to London. Outstanding grammar schools. Wide range of property types. Popular with families seeking excellent schools and reasonable commute times at more accessible prices than Sevenoaks and Tonbridge.

Canterbury

56 minutes to London St Pancras on the high-speed service. A world-class city in its own right with outstanding culture, schools and lifestyle. Popular with those who commute less frequently or work remotely.

Rochester and Medway

45 to 55 minutes to London Victoria and Charing Cross. Excellent value — one of the best in Kent for property prices and London access combined. The Medway towns are seeing significant regeneration and growing popularity with London buyers.

Property values across Kent

Property values vary significantly across Kent. Sevenoaks is the most expensive with average prices of £700,000 to £1.2 million for family homes. Rochester and the Medway towns are the most affordable at £250,000 to £400,000 for family homes. Maidstone, Canterbury and Tonbridge sit in the middle range at £350,000 to £600,000 for typical family properties.

What to expect on moving day from London to Kent

A London to Kent move typically takes between five and nine hours depending on the specific locations and volume. The M25, A2 and M2 are the main routes for most London to Medway and North Kent moves. The M25 and A21 serve West Kent. We recommend early morning starts to avoid peak traffic on all London to Kent routes.
