Moving from London to Kent — the complete guide for 2025

📅 10 May 2026 🕐 6 min read 👥 Medway & Kent Removals Team

If you're considering moving from London to Kent in 2025, you're joining thousands of families who are discovering the benefits of life in England's Garden County. With its perfect blend of rural charm, excellent transport links, and significantly more affordable housing, Kent offers an attractive alternative to London living without sacrificing convenience. Whether you're drawn to the historic charm of Rochester, the coastal beauty of Kent's seaside towns, or the excellent schools and family-friendly communities throughout the county, this comprehensive guide will help you navigate every aspect of your relocation from London to Kent.

Why People Are Moving from London to Kent in 2025

The trend of relocating to Kent from London has accelerated significantly, and 2025 continues to see record numbers of families making this move. The reasons are compelling and varied, from economic benefits to lifestyle improvements that Kent uniquely offers.

Cost of Living Benefits

One of the primary drivers for moving from London to Kent is the substantial difference in living costs. Property prices in Kent towns like Rochester, Chatham, and Gillingham typically offer 30-40% more space for your money compared to similar properties in London. This means families can upgrade from a cramped London flat to a spacious family home with a garden, often while reducing their monthly mortgage payments.

Beyond housing, daily expenses from groceries to dining out are generally more affordable in Kent. Parking is often free or significantly cheaper than London rates, and council tax rates across Kent are typically lower than most London boroughs, providing ongoing savings that add up considerably over time.

Transport Links and Commuting Options

Kent's excellent transport infrastructure makes it an ideal choice for those who still need to commute to London occasionally or regularly. The High Speed 1 service connects St Pancras International to several Kent stations, including Ebbsfleet International, in under 20 minutes. Traditional rail services from towns like Rochester provide direct connections to London Victoria, London Bridge, and other major London terminals.

For those working flexibly or remotely, Kent offers the perfect balance of accessibility without the daily grind of London commuting. The M25, M20, and A2 provide excellent road links for occasional trips to the capital, while day-to-day life can be enjoyed in Kent's more relaxed environment.

Quality of Life Improvements

The lifestyle benefits of relocating to Kent from London are immediately apparent to most families. Children can attend schools with larger playing fields and often smaller class sizes. Weekend activities shift from crowded parks to exploring historic castles, beautiful countryside walks, and charming market towns.

The Medway area, including Rochester, offers particular appeal with its rich maritime history, stunning cathedral, and vibrant cultural scene, all while maintaining a strong sense of community that can be harder to find in London's urban environment.

Most Popular Kent Destinations for London Relocators

Different areas of Kent appeal to different types of relocators, depending on their priorities, budget, and lifestyle preferences. Understanding these popular destinations can help you identify the area that best suits your family's needs.

Rochester and the Medway Towns

Rochester stands out as one of Kent's most attractive destinations for London relocators. This historic cathedral city combines period charm with modern amenities, offering excellent transport links to London while providing a genuine sense of community. The annual Rochester Dickens Festival and regular farmers' markets create a vibrant local culture that's distinctly different from London life.

The broader Medway area, including Chatham and Gillingham, provides excellent value for money with good schools, shopping facilities, and recreational opportunities. Chatham's Historic Dockyard offers world-class attractions right on your doorstep, while the area's regeneration has brought new restaurants, cafes, and cultural venues.

Sevenoaks and West Kent

For those seeking premium living with excellent London commuting links, Sevenoaks and the surrounding West Kent area represents the perfect compromise. Property prices reflect the desirability of the area, but the excellent schools, beautiful countryside, and fast rail connections to London make it particularly popular with professional families.

Canterbury and East Kent

Canterbury offers university town culture combined with stunning medieval architecture and proximity to Kent's beautiful coastline. For families relocating to Kent from London, Canterbury provides excellent secondary and higher education options, while coastal towns like Whitstable and Herne Bay offer seaside living within easy reach.

Maidstone and Central Kent

As Kent's county town, Maidstone offers excellent shopping, dining, and cultural facilities while maintaining reasonable property prices. The surrounding villages provide rural living with good access to both Maidstone's amenities and London commuter routes.

Planning Your London to Kent Removal in 2025

Successfully moving from London to Kent requires careful planning and consideration of the unique challenges involved in relocating from urban to more rural or suburban environments.

Timing Your Move

The timing of your London to Kent removal can significantly impact both cost and convenience. Spring and early summer months are typically the busiest periods for relocating to Kent from London, as families prefer to move during school holidays and better weather. However, this increased demand can mean higher removal costs and less availability from removal companies.

Consider moving during quieter periods like late autumn or winter for better rates and more flexible scheduling. If you have school-age children, plan around academic terms to minimize disruption to their education.

Choosing the Right Removal Service

When moving from London to Kent, selecting a removal company with specific experience in this type of relocation is crucial. Look for companies that understand both London's access restrictions and parking challenges, as well as Kent's rural addresses and potential access issues.

A professional removal service should provide comprehensive packing services, insurance coverage, and storage options if needed. They should also be familiar with the most efficient routes between London and various Kent destinations, helping to minimize moving day stress and potential delays.

Decluttering Before Your Move

Moving from London to Kent presents an excellent opportunity to declutter and downsize possessions that may not suit your new lifestyle. Items that were essential for London living, such as multiple public transport cards or urban-specific equipment, may no longer be necessary.

Consider which belongings truly deserve space in your new Kent home. Many families find they can donate items to London charities before moving, reducing both removal costs and the time needed to settle into their new property.

Understanding Kent's Property Market in 2025

The Kent property market offers diverse options for London relocators, but understanding current trends and local variations is essential for making informed decisions.

Property Types and Prices

Kent's property market spans from converted oast houses and period cottages to modern developments and new-build estates. London relocators are often surprised by the variety of property types available, including character homes with large gardens that would be unaffordable in London.

Rochester and the Medway towns offer particularly good value, with Victorian terraces and Edwardian houses providing period character at accessible prices. New developments in areas like Chatham Maritime provide modern living with excellent amenities and transport links.

School Catchment Areas

Many families relocating to Kent from London prioritize school catchment areas when choosing their new home. Kent maintains a selective secondary school system in many areas, with grammar schools providing excellent educational opportunities. However, comprehensive schools throughout the county also achieve strong results.

Research school catchment boundaries carefully, as these can significantly impact both property prices and your children's educational options. The Kent County Council website provides detailed catchment information, but local knowledge from experienced estate agents can be invaluable.

Future Development Plans

Kent continues to see significant investment in infrastructure and development. The Lower Thames Crossing project will improve transport links, while various regeneration projects across towns like Rochester and Chatham are enhancing local amenities and property values.

Consider how planned developments might affect your chosen area, both in terms of improved amenities and potential changes to the local character that initially attracted you to relocate to Kent.

Practical Considerations for Your Move

Moving from London to Kent involves numerous practical considerations that extend beyond simply transporting your belongings from one location to another.

Updating Your Address

The process of updating your address when moving from London to Kent requires systematic organization to ensure nothing important is missed. Start with essential services like banks, insurance companies, and your employer, then work through utilities, subscriptions, and online accounts.

Don't forget to update your address with HM Revenue and Customs, the DVLA for your driving licence and vehicle registration, and your electoral registration. Many services can be updated online, but some may require phone calls or postal correspondence.

Healthcare and Services

Registering with new healthcare providers is essential when relocating to Kent from London. Research local GP practices in your new area and register as soon as possible, as some popular practices may have waiting lists. You'll also need to consider dental care, opticians, and any specialist healthcare services you or your family require.

Other essential services to research include local utility providers, waste collection schedules, and council services. Kent County Council provides comprehensive information about local services, but speaking to new neighbors can provide valuable insights into the best local providers.

Transportation Changes

Your transportation needs will likely change significantly when moving from London to Kent. While public transport remains available, many families find they rely more heavily on private vehicles for daily activities. Consider whether you need to purchase a car or upgrade your current vehicle for rural Kent driving.

Research local transport options including bus routes, train services, and cycling infrastructure. Many Kent towns have excellent walking and cycling routes that provide healthy alternatives to driving for local journeys.

Settling into Kent Life

Successfully relocating to Kent from London involves more than simply moving your possessions - it requires adapting to a different pace of life and building new community connections.

Building Community Connections

Kent communities often have stronger neighborhood connections than typical London areas. Local parish councils, community groups, and voluntary organizations provide excellent opportunities to meet neighbors and contribute to your new community.

In areas like Rochester, local historical societies, arts groups, and sports clubs offer varied ways to engage with community life. Many communities have active social media groups that provide helpful information about local events, recommendations, and services.

Exploring Your New Area

Take time to explore Kent's diverse attractions and amenities. From historic sites like Rochester Cathedral and Canterbury Cathedral to natural areas like the Kent Downs Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, there's always something new to discover.

Local farmers' markets, farm shops, and seasonal events provide opportunities to experience Kent's agricultural heritage and connect with local producers. These experiences often highlight the lifestyle improvements that drew you to relocate to Kent in the first place.

Maintaining London Connections

Many people worry about losing touch with London friends and cultural opportunities when moving to Kent. However, Kent's excellent transport links make it easy to maintain important London connections while enjoying the benefits of county living.

Plan regular visits to London for cultural events, social occasions, or work commitments. Many Kent residents find they appreciate London's attractions more as occasional visitors than they did as daily residents dealing with urban stresses.
