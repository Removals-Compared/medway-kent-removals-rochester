How to get the best removal quote — 10 questions to ask every company

📅 20 May 2026 🕐 6 min read 👥 Medway & Kent Removals Team

Getting the right removal quote can make the difference between a smooth, stress-free move and an expensive nightmare filled with hidden costs and unexpected surprises. Whether you're moving within Kent, from Rochester to Canterbury, or relocating from Medway to anywhere across the UK, asking the right questions upfront will help you secure the best removal quote and avoid costly mistakes later.

As a removal company serving Rochester, Chatham, Gillingham, and the wider Kent area, we've seen countless customers who chose solely based on price, only to discover their bargain quote came with strings attached. Here are the ten essential questions you should ask every removal company to ensure you get the best value, not just the cheapest price.

Why Getting Multiple Quotes Matters in Kent's Removal Market

The removal industry in Kent is competitive, with companies ranging from one-man operations to large national chains. Prices can vary dramatically between Rochester-based firms and those operating from London or other areas. However, the cheapest quote rarely represents the best value, especially when you factor in experience, insurance coverage, and local knowledge of Kent's unique challenges.

Many Medway residents have learned this lesson the hard way. A removal that seems like a bargain can quickly become expensive when you add fuel surcharges, stair charges, parking fees, or worse, when items get damaged due to inadequate insurance or inexperienced crew members.

Question 1: What Exactly Does Your Quote Include?

This might seem obvious, but you'd be surprised how many removal quotes lack crucial details. A proper quote should clearly state what's included in the price and what might cost extra.

Essential Inclusions to Look For

  • Number of crew members and their experience level
  • Size and type of removal vehicle
  • Loading and unloading services
  • Basic packing materials like blankets and straps
  • Fuel costs and mileage
  • Insurance coverage details

When we provide quotes for moves around Rochester and Medway, we always break down exactly what's included. This transparency helps you compare like-for-like quotes rather than guessing what additional costs might appear on moving day.

Question 2: Are There Any Additional Charges I Should Know About?

Hidden charges are the biggest complaint we hear from customers who've had bad experiences with other removal companies. Common additional charges that might not appear in your initial quote include:

  • Stair charges for properties without ground-floor access
  • Long carry charges if the van can't park close to your property
  • Congestion or parking charges in areas like Canterbury or Maidstone town centres
  • Fuel surcharges if diesel prices fluctuate significantly
  • Waiting time if you're not ready when the crew arrives
  • Storage charges if there are delays with your new property

Reputable removal companies serving Kent will be upfront about these potential charges during the quotation process. If a company can't give you clear answers about additional fees, consider this a red flag.

Question 3: What Insurance Coverage Do You Provide?

Insurance is one area where cheaper removal quotes often cut corners, leaving you vulnerable if something goes wrong during your move from Medway to your new home.

Types of Insurance Coverage

Most removal companies offer different levels of insurance coverage:

  • Basic goods in transit insurance: Usually covers around £20-40 per kilogram of damaged items
  • All risks insurance: Provides new-for-old replacement value
  • Full value protection: Covers the complete replacement cost of damaged items

For valuable items like antiques, artwork, or expensive electronics, basic coverage might not be sufficient. Ask specifically about coverage limits and whether you need to declare high-value items separately.

Question 4: How Long Have You Been Operating in Kent?

Experience matters enormously in the removal industry. Companies with years of experience serving Rochester, Gravesend, Dartford, and surrounding areas understand local challenges that newcomers might miss.

Experienced Kent removal companies know which streets in historic areas like Rochester's old town have access restrictions, where parking can be problematic in Chatham, and how to handle the narrow lanes common in many Kent villages. This local knowledge can prevent delays and additional charges on moving day.

Don't hesitate to ask about their experience with moves similar to yours. If you're moving from a Victorian terrace in Gillingham to a modern development in Medway, have they handled similar properties before?

Question 5: Can You Provide Recent Local References?

A reputable removal company should be happy to provide references from recent customers, particularly those in your local area. Speaking to someone who recently moved from Rochester to Maidstone, or from Chatham to Canterbury, can give you valuable insights into what to expect.

When contacting references, ask specific questions about:

  • Whether the crew arrived on time and worked efficiently
  • If there were any unexpected charges
  • How carefully items were handled
  • Whether the quote accurately reflected the final cost
  • How any problems or concerns were resolved

Online reviews can be helpful, but personal references often provide more detailed and honest feedback about the removal experience.

Question 6: What Happens If There Are Delays or Problems?

Moving day doesn't always go according to plan. Property chains can collapse, access issues can arise, or weather conditions might cause problems. Ask potential removal companies how they handle these common scenarios.

Key Areas to Discuss

  • What happens if your completion is delayed by several hours or days?
  • Do they offer secure storage facilities if needed?
  • How do they handle weather-related delays?
  • What's their policy on rescheduling at short notice?

Companies experienced in Kent house moves understand that property transactions can be unpredictable. They should have clear policies for handling delays and storage solutions available locally in the Medway area.

Question 7: Do You Handle Packing and Unpacking Services?

If you're considering packing services, get detailed information about what's included. Some companies offer basic packing of fragile items, while others provide complete pack-and-unpack services.

Important questions about packing services include:

  • Do they provide all packing materials?
  • Are fragile items like china and glassware given special attention?
  • Can they pack items the day before the move?
  • Do they offer unpacking services at your new Kent home?
  • What's the cost difference between full-service packing and basic protection?

For busy families moving within Medway or relocating to other parts of Kent, professional packing services can be worth the investment, but make sure you understand exactly what you're paying for.

Question 8: What Size Team and Vehicle Will You Provide?

The size of the crew and vehicle significantly impacts both the efficiency of your move and the final cost. A larger team might cost more per hour but could complete your move much faster, potentially saving money overall.

Factors That Determine Team Size

  • Size of your property and amount of furniture
  • Distance of the move within or from Kent
  • Access restrictions at either property
  • Time constraints for completion

For a typical three-bedroom house move in Rochester or Medway, a two-person crew with a large van is usually sufficient. However, if you have particularly heavy items like pianos or safes, or if access is challenging, a larger crew might be necessary.

Question 9: Are Your Crew Members Employed or Subcontracted?

This question reveals a lot about how a removal company operates and the level of service you can expect. Companies using employed staff typically have more control over training, standards, and accountability.

Employed crew members are generally:

  • Better trained in proper lifting and packing techniques
  • More familiar with company procedures and standards
  • Covered by the company's insurance policies
  • More accountable for their work quality

While some companies successfully use subcontractors, employed staff often provide more consistent service quality, which is particularly important for long-distance moves from Kent or valuable item transportation.

Question 10: What's Your Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy?

Property transactions can be unpredictable, especially in active markets like those around Maidstone, Canterbury, and coastal Kent areas. Understanding a company's flexibility regarding changes is crucial.

Important Policy Points

  • How much notice is required for cancellations without penalty?
  • What fees apply for rescheduling at short notice?
  • Do they offer provisional booking options?
  • Can you secure a date with a small deposit?

Companies experienced in Kent's property market understand that completion dates can shift, and should offer reasonable flexibility while still protecting their business interests.

Comparing Quotes: Beyond the Bottom Line

Once you've asked these questions to several removal companies, you'll have the information needed to make an informed comparison. Remember that the best removal quote for your move from or within Kent isn't necessarily the cheapest – it's the one that offers the best value considering:

  • Included services and potential additional charges
  • Insurance coverage levels
  • Company experience and reputation
  • Flexibility and customer service quality
  • Local knowledge and expertise

Red Flags to Watch For When Seeking Removal Quotes

While gathering quotes for your Kent move, be wary of companies that:

  • Provide quotes without seeing your property or getting detailed information
  • Demand large upfront payments before moving day
  • Can't provide proof of insurance or licensing
  • Give vague answers about additional charges
  • Refuse to provide written quotes or contracts
  • Have no local address or established Kent presence

Getting the Most Accurate Quote Possible

To ensure you receive accurate quotes for your Kent move, provide removal companies with detailed, honest information about:

  • Exact addresses for both properties
  • Complete inventory of items being moved
  • Access restrictions or parking limitations
  • Any fragile or high-value items requiring special care
  • Your preferred moving date and any flexibility
  • Additional services you might need

The more accurate information you provide, the more precise your quotes will be, reducing the likelihood of unexpected costs on moving day.

Local Considerations for Kent Moves

When getting quotes for moves within Kent or from the Medway area, make sure companies understand local challenges such as:

  • Historic property access restrictions in areas like Rochester and Canterbury
  • Parking limitations in town centres
  • Narrow country lanes in rural Kent locations
  • Potential traffic congestion around major routes like the M25 and A2
  • Seasonal considerations, especially for coastal areas

Companies with extensive Kent experience will factor these considerations into their quotes, while those unfamiliar with the area might underestimate the time and effort required.

Making Your Final Decision

After asking these ten questions and comparing responses from different companies, you should have a clear picture of which removal company offers the best value for your specific needs. The right choice will depend on your priorities, whether that's price, service level, local expertise, or peace of mind through comprehensive insurance.

Remember that a good removal company should welcome these questions and provide clear, detailed answers. If any company seems evasive or unwilling to provide the information you need, that's a strong indicator to look elsewhere for your Kent move.
