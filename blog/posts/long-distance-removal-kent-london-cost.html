Long Distance Removals from Kent to London: Costs and What to Expect

Published 17 Jun 2025  |  7 min read  |  Medway & Kent Removals

Long distance removals between Kent and London are one of the most common move types we carry out at Medway and Kent Removals. Whether you are moving from Rochester to a London borough, from Maidstone to South East London or from anywhere in Kent to any London postcode, this guide covers what to expect on costs, timing and planning.

What does a Kent to London removal cost?

The cost of a long distance removal between Kent and London depends on several factors — the volume of your belongings, the specific locations at both ends, access challenges and whether packing services are included. As a guide:

  • One-bedroom flat — Kent to London: approximately £350 to £550
  • Two-bedroom property — Kent to London: approximately £500 to £750
  • Three-bedroom house — Kent to London: approximately £700 to £1,100
  • Four-bedroom house — Kent to London: approximately £900 to £1,400

These are guide prices only. The actual cost depends on the specific properties involved and any access challenges. Medway and Kent Removals provides a fixed price based on a pre-move survey — the price we quote is the price you pay.

What affects the cost of a Kent to London move?

  • Volume of belongings — the primary cost driver. More belongings require larger vehicles and more crew.
  • Access at the London end — parking suspensions, high floors with no lift, narrow streets all add cost and time.
  • Congestion Charge — moves into the central London zone attract the Congestion Charge of £15 per day per vehicle.
  • Distance — Dartford to East London is significantly shorter than Folkestone to West London.
  • Packing service — professional packing adds cost but eliminates the most time-consuming part of the move.

How long does a Kent to London move take?

A typical one or two-bedroom property move from Kent to London takes between four and seven hours. A three or four-bedroom house takes between six and ten hours. The drive time between Kent and London — typically 45 minutes to 90 minutes depending on origin and destination — is additional to loading and unloading time. Early starts are essential to avoid peak traffic on the M2, M25 and A2.

London parking suspensions

Most London streets have controlled parking zones or yellow lines that prevent a removal vehicle from parking legally without a formal suspension. Parking suspensions from the relevant London borough typically cost £50 to £120 per bay per day and require five to ten working days notice. Medway and Kent Removals manages parking suspension applications for all London delivery addresses as part of our service.

Book London moves on weekday mornings where possible

Peak hours on the M2, M25 and A2 from Kent into London are 7am to 9am and 4pm to 7pm. We recommend loading from the Kent property before 7am to arrive in London before 9am, or loading mid-morning to arrive in London mid-afternoon.
