Moving to Maidstone: A Complete Guide for New Residents

Published 13 May 2025  |  8 min read  |  Medway & Kent Removals

Maidstone is Kent's county town and one of the most popular relocation destinations in the county. With good London commute times via the M20 and Southeastern rail, outstanding grammar schools, a strong independent town centre and a wide range of property types, Maidstone attracts families, professionals and first-time buyers from across London and the wider South East.

Why people choose Maidstone

The financial argument for Maidstone is compelling. A budget of £400,000 that buys a one-bedroom flat in Zone 3 London buys a three-bedroom family house with a garden in many parts of Maidstone. The grammar school system is a major draw for families — Maidstone Grammar School for Boys and Invicta Grammar School for Girls are both outstanding. The M20 motorway at junction 7 provides fast road access to London and the M25.

Best areas for new residents

Bearsted and Thurnham

The most popular area for families relocating to Maidstone — a semi-rural village setting east of the town with its own railway station, outstanding primary schools and easy access to the North Downs. Property is predominantly larger detached and semi-detached family homes. Premium prices but outstanding value against London equivalents.

Loose and Boughton Monchelsea

Beautiful village settings south of Maidstone with period properties and excellent primary schools. Loose in particular has a stunning village centre with a stream running through it. Popular with buyers seeking character alongside practicality.

Shepway and Senacre

More affordable residential areas on the southern outskirts of Maidstone. Good primary schools and easy motorway access. Popular with first-time buyers and young families.

Commuting from Maidstone

Maidstone East and Maidstone West stations offer Southeastern services to London Victoria in approximately 65 to 75 minutes. The M20 motorway provides road access to the M25 and London in under an hour in normal traffic. Annual season tickets cost approximately £5,200 to £5,800.

Schools in Maidstone

Maidstone Grammar School for Boys and Invicta Grammar School for Girls are both outstanding selective state schools. Valley Park School and Cornwallis Academy are the main comprehensive options. Primary school provision includes several Outstanding rated schools. The educational offer in Maidstone is one of the strongest in Kent.

Moving day to Maidstone — what to expect

The M20 at junction 7 and the A229 through Maidstone can be congested during peak hours. We recommend early starts for all Maidstone removals to avoid peak traffic. Village properties in Bearsted and Loose sometimes have narrow lane access — we always survey and select the right vehicle size for these locations.

Apply for grammar schools before you move

If you have children approaching 11+ age, register for the Kent Test as early as possible. The test registration opens in the spring for the following September intake. Contact Kent County Council admissions for current deadlines.
