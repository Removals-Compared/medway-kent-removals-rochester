How to update your address when you move home — the complete list

📅 16 May 2026 🕐 6 min read 👥 Medway & Kent Removals Team

Moving home is exciting, but one of the most overwhelming tasks is updating your address with every organisation that needs to know. Whether you're relocating within Rochester, moving from Chatham to Gillingham, or leaving the Medway area entirely, you'll need to notify dozens of companies and services. Many people forget crucial contacts and end up missing important mail or facing service interruptions. This detailed checklist will help you tackle your address change systematically, ensuring nothing gets overlooked during your Kent house move.

When to Start Updating Your Address

Start your address updates at least four weeks before your moving date. Some organisations require significant notice periods, particularly financial institutions and government departments. If you're moving within Kent, you might have shorter timeframes for local services, but it's better to be safe than sorry.

Create a spreadsheet or notebook dedicated to tracking your address changes. Include columns for the organisation name, contact method, date contacted, and confirmation received. This system prevents duplicate work and helps you follow up on any delays.

Essential Government and Legal Services

HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC)

Update your address with HMRC immediately to avoid issues with tax correspondence. You can change your address online through your personal tax account, by phone, or by post. If you're self-employed, remember to update both your personal and business addresses. Missing tax correspondence can lead to penalties, so make this a priority on your moving checklist.

DVLA and Vehicle Registration

You have 14 days to notify DVLA of your address change, whether you're moving from Rochester to Canterbury or anywhere else in the UK. Update both your driving licence and vehicle registration documents. You can do this online, which is the quickest method. Remember to update your car insurance before notifying DVLA, as they may check this information.

Electoral Register

Register to vote at your new address with the local council. In Kent, this might be Medway Council if you're staying in the Rochester, Chatham, or Gillingham areas, or another Kent council if you're moving elsewhere in the county. You can register online at gov.uk, and it's crucial for credit rating purposes as well as voting rights.

Passport Office

While you don't legally need to update your passport address immediately, doing so ensures any correspondence reaches you. You can update your details when you next renew your passport or contact the Passport Office directly if you need to change it sooner.

Financial Institutions and Credit

Banks and Building Societies

Contact every bank and building society where you hold accounts. Most offer online address changes through internet banking, but some may require phone calls or branch visits. Update current accounts, savings accounts, mortgages, loans, and credit cards separately, as they might be managed by different departments.

If you bank with a local Kent branch, such as those in Medway's high streets, consider visiting in person to ensure everything is updated correctly. Bring proof of your new address, such as a council tax bill or tenancy agreement.

Credit Reference Agencies

Update your address with all three major credit reference agencies: Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion. This ensures your credit file reflects your current address, which is vital for future credit applications. Many people forget this step and wonder why their credit applications are declined after moving.

Pension Providers

Don't forget workplace pensions, private pensions, and state pension services. Missing pension correspondence can affect your retirement planning and annual statements. Contact each provider separately, as they rarely share information.

Insurance and Protection Services

Home Insurance

Your home insurance must be updated before you move. If you're relocating within Kent, your premiums might not change significantly, but moving from a rural area near Maidstone to central Rochester could affect your rates. Contact your insurer well in advance to avoid any gaps in coverage.

Car Insurance

Vehicle insurance premiums can vary significantly based on location. Moving from a quiet village near Sittingbourne to busy Medway might increase your premiums, while the opposite move could reduce them. Update your car insurance before your move to ensure continuous coverage and accurate pricing.

Life and Health Insurance

Update all life insurance, critical illness cover, and private health insurance policies. While your location might not affect premiums for these products, ensuring they have your correct address prevents important policy documents from going astray.

Utilities and Home Services

Energy Suppliers

If you're staying with the same energy supplier at your new Kent property, contact them to transfer your account. If you're switching suppliers, ensure final readings are taken at your old property and opening readings at your new one. Many suppliers offer online moving services that handle transfers automatically.

Water Companies

In Kent, you'll likely be dealing with Southern Water or Thames Water depending on your exact location. Contact them to set up your new account and close your old one. If you're moving within the same water company area, they can often transfer your account seamlessly.

Telecoms and Internet

Book your internet and phone line installation well in advance, especially if you're moving to a new-build property in one of Kent's expanding areas. Some providers need several weeks' notice for installations. Consider whether you want to stay with your current provider or switch to better deals available at your new address.

Council Tax

Inform your current council of your moving date and contact your new council to set up council tax payments. If you're moving within Medway, this process is simpler as you'll stay with the same authority. However, moving from Medway to other parts of Kent means dealing with different councils with varying procedures and rates.

Subscription and Delivery Services

Postal Services

Set up mail redirection with Royal Mail well before your move. This safety net catches any mail sent to your old address during the transition period. Choose a redirection period that gives you time to update all your contacts, typically 6-12 months for a thorough transition.

Online Shopping Accounts

Update your address on all online shopping platforms including Amazon, supermarket delivery services, and any other retailers you use regularly. Many people forget about occasional-use accounts and then wonder why orders are delivered to their old address months later.

Subscription Services

Don't forget magazine subscriptions, streaming services with postal elements, meal delivery services, and any other regular deliveries. Even digital services may need address updates for billing and legal purposes.

Healthcare and Educational Services

NHS Services

Register with a new GP practice near your new home. In Kent, you'll want to find practices accepting new patients in your area, whether that's in Rochester, the surrounding Medway towns, or elsewhere in the county. Also update your address with dentists, opticians, and any specialist consultants you see regularly.

Schools and Colleges

If you have children, inform their schools about your address change. If you're moving to a different catchment area within Kent, you may need to apply for school transfers. Contact Kent County Council's education department for guidance on school moves within the county.

Employment and Professional Services

Employers

Inform your employer's HR department about your address change for payroll, emergency contacts, and other records. If you're moving further from your workplace, discuss any changes to commuting arrangements or flexible working options.

Professional Bodies

Update your address with any professional memberships, trade associations, or regulatory bodies relevant to your career. These organisations often send important updates and renewal notices that you can't afford to miss.

Local Services and Memberships

Libraries and Leisure Centres

If you're moving within Kent, you can often transfer library memberships between different areas. Medway libraries have slightly different systems from other Kent libraries, so check what transfers are available. Update gym memberships and consider whether you want to transfer to branches near your new home.

Religious Organisations

Inform your place of worship about your move. If you're staying within the same area of Kent, you might continue attending the same services, but they'll still need your updated address for their records.

Creating Your Personal Checklist

Every household is different, so create a personalised checklist based on your specific circumstances. Walk through your daily life and think about every organisation that sends you mail or needs to contact you. Check your emails for regular correspondence and note companies you might have forgotten.

Consider seasonal services too. If you're moving in summer, you might forget about winter services like heating maintenance contracts. If you're moving in winter, garden services and summer activity memberships might slip your mind.

Managing the Process Efficiently

Set aside dedicated time each week for address updates rather than trying to do everything at once. Some changes can be batched together, such as calling all your insurance providers on the same day when you have policy numbers readily available.

Keep a file of confirmation emails and reference numbers for all your address changes. This documentation helps if there are any disputes later or if services don't update correctly.

Don't assume that one part of a large organisation will tell other parts about your address change. Banks, in particular, often require separate notifications for different products even when they're all with the same provider.

What to Do If You Forget Someone

Even with the most thorough checklist, you'll probably forget to update someone. When mail arrives at your old address after Royal Mail redirection ends, or when you realise a service is still using your old details, don't panic. Contact them immediately to update your information.

Keep a note of any organisations you discover after your move so you can include them in future moving checklists. Many people move multiple times throughout their lives, so learning from each experience makes subsequent moves smoother.

Moving home doesn't have to be chaotic when you plan your address changes systematically. Whether you're relocating within Rochester, moving elsewhere in Kent, or leaving the area entirely, this checklist helps ensure you don't lose touch with important services and correspondence.
