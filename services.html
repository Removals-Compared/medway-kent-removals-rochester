🏠 House Removals Rochester and Kent

Professional House Removals in Rochester, Medway and All of Kent

Moving home is consistently rated as one of the most stressful life events a person can experience. At Medway and Kent Removals, our entire operation is designed to take that stress away from you and handle every detail of your move with care, efficiency and genuine professionalism. We have been carrying out house removals across Rochester, Medway and the whole of Kent since 2020, and we have completed over 800 successful residential moves in that time.

We handle moves of all sizes. Whether you are moving out of a studio flat in Strood or vacating a large 5-bedroom detached house in Maidstone, our approach is the same: we send the right crew, the right vehicle, and the right equipment for your specific job. We do not understaff moves to cut costs. We do not send a two-man team to a four-bedroom house. We take the time upfront to understand exactly what is involved and ensure we are properly resourced from the start.

Our removal crew arrives at the agreed time. They introduce themselves, carry out a brief walkthrough of your property to confirm the plan, and then begin work methodically. Furniture is wrapped in padded blankets before it moves. Fragile items are given extra protection. Floors and door frames are protected throughout the move to prevent damage to your property. We disassemble large items such as bed frames and wardrobes where necessary and reassemble them in your new home. We load the vehicle systematically to maximise safety and efficiency during transit, and we unload and place items in the correct rooms at your new property. We do not leave until you have confirmed you are happy with everything.

Our local knowledge of Medway means we plan routes carefully to avoid unnecessary delays. We know the parking restrictions in Rochester city centre, the one-way systems in Chatham, the access challenges in Gillingham's denser residential streets and the parking limitations around many Medway estates. This saves time on the day and means your move runs smoothly from start to finish.

What to expect on house removal day

The evening before your move, you will receive a confirmation call from our team to go through the day's plan and answer any last-minute questions. Our crew will arrive punctually on moving day, fully equipped and ready to work. If you have opted for our packing service, this is usually carried out the day before the main removal to ensure everything is properly prepared. On the day itself, we work efficiently and carefully, keeping you updated throughout. We bring our own equipment including blankets, straps, trolleys, wardrobe boxes, floor protection and all necessary tools for disassembly and reassembly.

We provide fully itemised, fixed-price quotes for all our house removals. There are no hidden charges, no moving-day surprises and no extra fees for stairs, parking waits or heavy items beyond what is stated in your quote. Whether you are relocating a small accounting firm from a serviced office in Rochester city centre, or moving a mid-sized business from a commercial unit in Chatham to new premises in Maidstone, we plan and execute the move to minimise downtime and disruption to your operations.

📦 Professional Packing Services Kent

Professional Packing Services for Home and Office Moves in Kent

Packing is without question the most labour-intensive and time-consuming part of any move. It is also the stage where the most damage occurs if done incorrectly. Inadequate wrapping of fragile items, overfilled boxes, incorrect stacking in the van, and poorly sealed containers are all common causes of damage during removals. Our professional packing service eliminates these risks entirely.

Our trained packing team in Rochester has packed hundreds of properties across Medway and Kent, from small one-bedroom flats to large family homes with extensive collections of antiques, artwork, fine china and fragile ornaments. We bring the right materials, we use the correct techniques for each type of item, and we work methodically to ensure every single item is safe before it is moved.

We offer three packing options to suit your needs and budget. Our full packing service means our team packs everything in your property, room by room, using the appropriate materials for each category of item. Our part-packing service means we pack specific rooms, particular types of items such as fragiles and kitchen contents, or items that you are not comfortable packing yourself. Our materials-only service means we supply you with a full set of professional-grade packing materials so you can pack yourself, with the confidence that you are using the same quality materials our professional team uses.

Specialist packing for fragile and valuable items

Fragile items require a completely different approach to standard household goods. At Medway and Kent Removals, our packers are trained in the specific techniques required for different categories of fragile items. China and glassware is individually wrapped in white tissue paper, nested correctly and surrounded by bubble wrap in double-walled boxes. Artwork and mirrors are wrapped in specialist mirror boxes or custom cardboard frames. Electronics are wrapped in antistatic materials and supported in correctly sized boxes. Ornaments and collectibles are individually wrapped and packed with void fill to prevent movement during transit.

Packing materials we use

Every packing service we carry out uses the same high-quality materials: double-walled cardboard boxes in small, medium, large and extra-large sizes, bubble wrap in multiple thicknesses, white acid-free tissue paper, foam wrap sheets, packing peanuts, specialist wardrobe boxes so your hanging clothes move on the hanger without creasing, mattress bags in all standard sizes, sofa and armchair covers, and TV boxes for large flat-screen televisions. All materials are included in the packing service price. You are not charged separately per box or per roll of bubble wrap.

What is included in our packing service

  • Double-walled cardboard boxes in all sizes, included in price
  • Bubble wrap, tissue paper, foam wrap and packing peanuts
  • Wardrobe boxes so clothes travel on the hanger
  • Mattress bags in all standard sizes
  • Sofa, armchair and large furniture covers
  • Mirror and artwork specialist packing
  • TV and electronics protective boxes and packaging
  • Clear labelling of every box with contents and destination room
  • Written inventory of packed boxes for your records
  • Unpacking service available at your new property on request
  • Full packing, part-packing or materials supply only available

Medway and Kent Removals offers flexible secure storage near our Rochester base for residential and commercial customers across Medway and Kent. Your belongings are stored in a secure, dry, clean and climate-controlled storage facility. Access is managed and monitored around the clock. 🗺 Long Distance Removals from Kent

Long Distance Removal Services from Kent to Anywhere in Great Britain

Medway and Kent Removals carries out long-distance removal services from Rochester and across the whole of Kent to any destination in mainland Great Britain. Whether you are relocating from Rochester to London, from Chatham to Manchester, from Maidstone to Edinburgh or from Gravesend to Cardiff, we provide the same professional, fully insured, fixed-price service you would expect from a local move.

Long-distance moves require a higher level of planning and preparation than local removals. Belongings need to be secured more thoroughly for longer journeys. Routes need to be planned carefully to accommodate vehicle size restrictions and traffic patterns. Loading needs to be even more systematic to prevent items from shifting during transit on motorways and dual carriageways. Our team has the experience and the equipment to handle all of this correctly.

We use larger vehicles for long-distance moves, typically 7.5-tonne or larger removal lorries that can accommodate the contents of most family homes in a single load. For very large properties or complex moves, we may use two vehicles. We discuss this with you upfront and reflect it accurately in your quote so there are no surprises.

Fixed-price long distance quotes with no surcharges

One of the most frustrating experiences people have with long-distance removal companies is receiving a low quote and then being hit with additional charges on moving day for fuel, mileage, additional crew time or other unexpected costs. At Medway and Kent Removals, your fixed-price quote covers everything we agreed. There are no fuel surcharges, no mileage additions, no motorway toll charges added on top, and no additional crew time charges. The price you are quoted is the price you pay.

Packing and storage for long distance moves

For long-distance moves, we strongly recommend considering our professional packing service. Items that are adequately packed for a 20-minute drive across Medway may not be adequately protected for a 5-hour drive to the North of England. Our packing team ensures every item is packed to withstand a long-distance journey, using additional internal padding, stronger taping of boxes, and more comprehensive wrapping of furniture and fragile items. If your new property is not yet ready at the time of your move from Kent, we can also offer interim storage at our Rochester facility while you wait for completion.
