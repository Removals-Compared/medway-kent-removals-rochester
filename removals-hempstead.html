Our local knowledge of Hempstead

Hempstead sits in a sheltered valley on the southern fringe of Gillingham, anchored by the Hempstead Valley shopping centre and the surrounding residential streets. The main road network is the A278 link road south to the M2 and east to Hoath Lane, with quieter estate roads serving the residential areas. For a removal lorry the road geometry is generally good, wide carriageways on the spines, predictable turning circles and reasonable visibility.

The Hempstead housing stock is overwhelmingly post-war and 1970s onwards: semi-detached and detached family homes, a smaller proportion of bungalows, and a good range of executive 4 and 5-bedroom properties on the more recent developments. Hempstead has a noticeable concentration of family buyers because of the catchments for the local schools and the easy M2 commute, which means many of our jobs are 3 to 4-bedroom family moves with reasonable driveway parking.

Parking restrictions are minimal, there are no permit zones, no pay-and-display areas in the residential streets, and most properties have driveway space. The only access point worth planning around is the area immediately surrounding Hempstead Valley shopping centre, which gets very busy at peak retail hours. We time loads to avoid Saturday lunchtimes and the Christmas shopping period.

Hempstead also handles a steady flow of long-distance moves linked to the M2 commuting market, with families coming in from London and outbound jobs to elsewhere in Kent and the wider South East. We handle the full mix as fixed-price quotes with extended journey insurance included as standard.

On every Hempstead move our team confirms the vehicle size in advance based on the property type, the access at both ends and the volume of contents. For 4 and 5-bedroom homes around Wigmore and the executive estates we routinely use a 7.5 tonne lorry; for the older residential closes near Twydall we plan with a Luton-bodied vehicle. We never add charges on the day for vehicle size, parking time or access decisions.

Hempstead moves frequently extend into the wider Medway towns, for the immediately adjacent communities we coordinate with our Gillingham removals service, and for cross-Medway jobs we run direct routes through our Chatham removals service coverage.

Did you know? Your home contents insurance almost certainly does not cover your belongings during a removal. Every move we carry out is protected by comprehensive goods-in-transit insurance covering full replacement value. We carry proof of cover on every job and provide it on request before you commit to a booking.

How we price your Hempstead removal

Every Hempstead quote we write is built from the actual specifics of your move, not a one-size-fits-all rate card. The factors that genuinely affect the price are the volume of your contents, the property type and access at both ends, the distance involved, the date you want to move, and any extras such as packing, furniture dismantling and reassembly, or storage. For 3-bedroom homes and larger we offer a free pre-move survey, in person or via a video walkthrough, so the quote is grounded in what is actually being moved rather than a guess from a postcode and a property size.

Once you accept the quote, the figure is fixed. We do not add charges on the day for stairs, parking waits, heavier items, additional time, fuel, or anything else.
